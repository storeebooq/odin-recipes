Sunshine Leom Cake

Description

This is the perfect cake for treating guests on a summer day.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (1/2 lb.) butter, softened
  • 1 3/4 cups granulated sugar
  • Finely chopped zest of 6 lemons
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp. baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 4 cups confectioners' sugar
  • Juice of 3 lemons

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Lightly butter and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  3. Cream 1/2 cup of the butter and the sugar in the bowl of an electric mixer until light and fluffy.
  4. Add half the zest and all the egg yolks, one at a time.
  5. Sift together the flour, baking powder and salt.
  6. Add the dry ingredients, in thirds, to the egg mixture, alternating with the milk, and ending with the last of the dry ingredients.
  7. Divide the batter into the prepared pans.
  8. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, until golden or the center springs back when touched.
  9. Place on a rack to cool for 10 minutes.
  10. Remove cakes from pans and place them onto rack to cool completely before frosting.
  11. Meanwhile, combine the remaining 1/2 cup butter, confectioners' sugar, remaining zest and the lemon juice until smooth.
  12. Frost and assemble the cake.

    13. Makes 1 X 9 - inch layer cake.
