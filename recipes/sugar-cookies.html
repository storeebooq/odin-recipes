Sugar Cookies


Description

Light, Sweet, Crisp and Delicious! These are some amazingly rich and tender cookies your family will love!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup soft shortening
  • 1/4 cup soft butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 Tbls. milk or cream
  • 1 tsp. flavoring (vanilla or lemon or a combination of the two)
  • 1 1/4 cup sifted flour
  • 1 tsp. cream of tartar
  • 1/2 tsp. baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp. salt

Directions

  1. Mix shortening, butter, sugar and egg together thoroughly.
  2. Stir in milk and flavoring.
  3. Sift dry ingredients together and stir in.
  4. Chill dough.
  5. Roll very thin (1/16"). Cut into desired shapes.
  6. Place on lightly greased baking sheet, sprinkle with sugar.
  7. Bake at 425 F for 5-7 minutes, until delicately browned.
